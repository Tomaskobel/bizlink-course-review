Step 1

Group Repeated Corrections

When multiple reviewers pointed out the same issue across different lessons, we extracted it as a course-wide rule that applies everywhere.

Step 2

Classify & Prioritize

Every action tagged on two axes: what kind (terminology, technical, content, tone, visual) and how urgent (P1 critical to P4 nice-to-have).

Step 3

Apply in the Right Order

First fix safety/technical issues, then terminology, then content rewrites, finally tone polish.

Why? A terminology fix in Step 1 may resolve 6 lesson actions at once, saving rework.

How to review this lesson

Each card below is a specific change proposed by an SME reviewer. For each one, you have three options:
